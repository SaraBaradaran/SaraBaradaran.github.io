I am a third-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California (USC), advised by [Dr. Mukund Raghothaman](https://r-mukund.github.io). My research interests lie broadly in software engineering and programming languages. I previously earned a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the Isfahan University of Technology (IUT) and an M.S. in Computer Science from USC.
